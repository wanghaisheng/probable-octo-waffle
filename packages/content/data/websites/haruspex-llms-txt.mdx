# Haruspex

AI-powered investment intelligence platform that analyzes stocks across 21 dimensions including fundamentals, geopolitics, supply chain resilience, and market sentiment. Generates daily Haruspex Scores (0-100) for hundreds of publicly traded stocks, with daily market roundups, stock deep dives, and sector analysis published in 9 languages.

## Key Features

- Multi-dimensional stock analysis (21 scoring dimensions)
- Daily Haruspex Scores (0-100) for hundreds of stocks
- Market roundups, deep dives, and sector analysis
- Available in 9 languages

## About llms.txt Implementation

Haruspex provides both `llms.txt` and `llms-full.txt` files detailing the platform's capabilities, scoring methodology, and available financial data.
